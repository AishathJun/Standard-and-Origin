About Us

We assure that we will provide you with the highest quality products.

Standard & Origin Pvt Ltd, also known as S&O was established on 23rd February 2011, and it is now one of the Maldives' fastest-growing firms, specializing in the supply, and delivery of temperature-controlled products such as frozen fruits, vegetables & meat, predominantly to the Hotel & Resort industries.

We started small but has now grown over the last 10 years with approximately 3 physical stores and serve over 90% of Hotels and Resorts in the Maldives, including Four Season Resorts, Six Senses Laamu, and Hilton Resorts.
